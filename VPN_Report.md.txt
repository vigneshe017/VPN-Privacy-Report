| Feature               | Details                                    |
|------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| **Protocol**          | OpenVPN (UDP)                              |
| **Encryption**        | AES-256 encryption                         |
| **Privacy**           | No-logs policy, DNS leak protection, IP masking |
| **Tunnel**           | Encrypted tunnel between device & VPN server |

## 8. Benefits of VPN
- ✅ **Enhances Privacy:** Hides real IP address from websites.  
- 🔐 **Secure Traffic:** Encrypts data to prevent eavesdropping.  
- 🌍 **Bypasses Restrictions:** Access content not available in some regions.  
- ☕ **Safe Public Wi-Fi Usage:** Protects data on open networks.

---

## 9. Limitations of VPN
- 📉 **Speed Reduction:** VPN may cause slower browsing due to encryption overhead.  
- 🌐 **Limited Servers:** Free VPN plans have fewer server options.  
- 📝 **Trust Factor:** Users must trust the VPN provider’s no-logs policy.  
- 🚫 **Not a Complete Shield:** VPNs don’t prevent all types of attacks (e.g., phishing).

---

## 10. Conclusion
VPNs play a critical role in protecting user privacy and ensuring secure communication online. By encrypting traffic and masking IP addresses, they help prevent unauthorized access and tracking.
